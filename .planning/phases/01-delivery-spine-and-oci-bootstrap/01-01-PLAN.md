| nginx->next-app | Reverse-proxy traffic crosses from the public entrypoint to the private app container |

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |

| T-01-02 | D | `deploy/compose/compose.prod.yaml` | mitigate | Keep the app container off the public port map so traffic must traverse nginx. |
| T-01-03 | I | `app/app/page.tsx` | accept | The proof page is intentionally non-sensitive and should not include secrets or OCI metadata. |
</threat_model>
